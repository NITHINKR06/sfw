The fieldset element in HTML5 is used to group related elements within a form. It's helpful for 
organizing and visually distinguishing form sections. It’s often paired with the legend element, 
which  provides  a  caption  or  title  for  the  group.  Here's  a  simple  example: 
<fieldset><legend>Personal Information</legend>...</fieldset>. This helps improve readability 
and accessibility.
